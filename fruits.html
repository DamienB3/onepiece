Devil Fruits

Devil Fruits are rare, mystical fruits that grant the eater of the fruit a secret ability. However, the cost of the fruit means that the eater will never be able to swim, and possibly altering their bodies in unexpected ways.

    Logia

    Logia devil fruits allow any user who eats the fruit the ability to alter their body into a natural element.

    Zoan

    Those who eat a Zoan devil fruit will learn that their new power allows them to turn into an animal, whether real or mystical.

    Paramecia

    Paramecia powers vastly range in the ways the user can manipulate their body, or environment.
