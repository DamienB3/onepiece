A beginner's guide to the world of One Piece:

One Piece is an Anime (Japanese cartoon) that started in Japan as a manga (Japanese comic book) in 1997 by Eiichiro Oda, and since then has taken off in many parts of the world, including America.

The story follows a young boy named Monkey D. Luffy (Luffy for short) as he travels around the world in his quest to become the greatest pirate ever, and claim his throne as the "Pirate King". However, he will need to battle with other Pirates who also desire to be the King, the law enforcement, and even citizens who are against pirates in general.
